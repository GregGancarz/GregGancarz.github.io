Accountable

Accountable is an expense logging and tracking app designed to help the economically inclined user keep track of their expenses and spending habits. The MERN stack app features complete CRUD functionality within React. Due to the individualistic nature of the app, an email is required to sign-up. Currently, users will receive a welcome email upon sign up.

History Quest

History Quest is a single and multi-player history multiple-choice quiz game. The questions are generated from a pre-set selection, but never repeat in the same round. The build is created with only JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Greg's List

Greg's List is a full CRUD posting app designed to serve as a hub for tech industry. It includes categories for job listings, meet-ups, and prospective hires. Features Multiple model interconnectedness and bcrypt login/sign-up functionality..

CannaBase

CannaBase is a full CRUD React app built with the MERN stack. Consuming a 3rd party API, the app allows users to search for and discover various varieties of cannabis based on what medical ailments they are seeking relief from, among other criteria.
